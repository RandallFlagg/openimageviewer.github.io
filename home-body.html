About
Open Image Viewer is a hardware accelerated, light-weight and stand-alone open code image viewer.
A prominent guideline of Open Image Viewer is attributed to Human factors and ergonomics by optimizing input (mouse / keyboard) and output (monitor) to the human physiology, thus doing more with less.
The Viewer aims to be the status-quo in viewing and analyzing images with no compromise in performance, comfort or universality. The project is built from scratch using modern techniques, modern libraries and non legacy constraints.
Off-the-shelf supported formats
Bmp, CUT (Dr. Halo), DDS, EXR, GIF, Gif, HDR, ICO, IFF, JNG, JPEG, JPEG-XR, JPEG/JIF, Jpeg 2000, KOALA, MNG, PBM/PGM/PPM, PCX, PFM , PICT, PSD, PhotoCD, Png, RAS , RAW , Raw Fax G3, SGI , TARGA , Tiff 16 bit float, Tiff 16 bit signed integer, Tiff 24 bit float Tiff 32 bit float, WBMP , WebP, XBM , XPM,
